Writer | Narrative Designer

Hi There!

I'm Amruth Varshan, a designer at Zynga, and I craft engaging narratives for games played by millions of players around the world. I work on iconic IPs such Willy Wonka, and The Wizard of Oz, expanding the world and lore of the wildly popular franchises. Most prominently, I've created content capable of sustaining a live game for 3+ years.

My fascination with interactivity and love for compelling storytelling have led me to the games industry, where I'm constantly discovering new possibilities and learning new skills. At Zynga, I also design levels, and assist in the creation of gameplay features. While my skillset may initially appear random, it lets accomplish any task assigned to me.

With a functional literacy for game engines & programming, and a demonstrable history of skill with writing, I'm in a unique position to tackle any challenge that the nebulous endeavour of games development might throw at me. I hope to one day direct games of my own.
