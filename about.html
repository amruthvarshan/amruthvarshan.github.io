Game & Narrative Designer

Hi There!

Hi there! I'm Amruth Varshan, currently a level designer at Rovio. Over the course of my career, I have contributed to IPs such as Tom Clancy's Ghost Recon, Willy Wonka, and The Wizard of Oz. I've worked on expanding the lore and world of these iconic franchises in meaningful ways.

I have experience with designing features & content for live games, as well as production & testing on console games. With a functional literacy for all verticals in the game development pipeline, my skills as a generalist help me learn and improve rapidly as a designer. I aim to one day direct games of my own.
