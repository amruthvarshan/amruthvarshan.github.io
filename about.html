Amruth Varshan

Game Writer | Narrative Designer

Hi There!

I'm Amruth Varshan, currently a designer at Zynga where I craft engaging narratives for games played by millions of players around the world. I work on iconic IPs such as Willy Wonka and The Wizard of Oz, expanding the world and lore of the wildly popular franchises. Most notably, I've singlehandedly created content capable of sustaining a live game for 3+ years.

My fascination with interactivity and love for compelling storytelling have led me to the games industry, where I'm constantly discovering new possibilities and learning new skills. At Zynga, I also design levels, in addition to the creation of gameplay features. While my skillset - which includes but isn't limited to writing, scripting, narrative & game design, research, QA, documentation, level design - may initially appear random, it lets me accomplish tasks in a wide array of verticals.

With a functional literacy for game engines & programming, and a demonstrable history of skill with writing, I'm in a unique position to tackle any challenge that the nebulous endeavour of games development might have on offer. I hope to one day direct games of my own.
