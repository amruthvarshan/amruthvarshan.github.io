About

I'm Amruth Varshan, currently a designer at Zynga where I craft engaging narratives for games played by millions of players around the world. I work on iconic IPs such as Willy Wonka and The Wizard of Oz, expanding the world and lore of the wildly popular franchises. Most notably, I've singlehandedly created content capable of sustaining a live game for 3+ years.
